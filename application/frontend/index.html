Visual Known-Item Search

Welcome!
In this study, your task is to find a specific target image from a series of image sets. Please read the instructions below on how to navigate and complete this study.

Instructions:

  1. Target Image:
    The target image is displayed before each set of images is shown. You can also view the target image anytime by clicking the
    Toggle Target Image
    button located at the top left of the screen.
  2. Comparing Images:
    If you want to directly compare an image in the set to the target image, click on the image you want to compare the target to.
  3. Submitting an Image:
    After locating the image corresponding to the target image, click the green
    Submit
    button on the image to make your selection. If your choice is incorrect, you'll have a chance to try again!
  4. Progress:
    You can track your progress on the bar at the top of the screen, which includes all control buttons and shows your position within the study.
  5. Skipping an Image Set:
    If you get stuck finding the image, you may use a
    Skip
    button that will appear in the top right after 30 seconds to allow you to move forward. (It's possible for target image to not be in the set.)

Additional Information:

  • Note that each image set may take a few seconds to load.
  • Data collection:
    Your scroll position and button clicks will be tracked, but all data is collected anonymously.
